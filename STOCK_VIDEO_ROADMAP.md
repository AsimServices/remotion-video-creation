# What Videos to Produce: Stock Video Content Roadmap

## Context

You have a Remotion setup capable of creating programmatic animated videos. This roadmap covers **what types of videos to actually produce** — the themes, visuals, and content categories that have the highest commercial value on Adobe Stock, Shutterstock, Pond5, and Storyblocks.

---

## TIER 1 — Top Sellers (Produce These First)

### 1. Abstract Geometric Backgrounds (Looping)

The single most in-demand stock video category. These are neutral, non-distracting animated backgrounds.

**Specific videos to produce:**

| # | Video Theme | Visual Description | Who Buys It |
|---|------------|-------------------|-------------|
| 1 | **Network Constellation** | Floating dots connected by thin lines on dark background, slowly drifting | Tech companies, AI startups, cybersecurity firms |
| 2 | **Hexagonal Grid Pulse** | Honeycomb pattern with ripple waves passing through, subtle glow | Biotech, science, engineering presentations |
| 3 | **Rotating Polygon Rings** | Concentric circles of triangles/hexagons rotating at different speeds | Conference stage screens, corporate events |
| 4 | **Dot Wave Grid** | Flat grid of dots rising and falling like ocean waves | SaaS websites, app landing pages |
| 5 | **Floating Glass Circles** | Translucent circles at different depths, slow parallax drift | Wellness, beauty, luxury brands |
| 6 | **Minimal Line Weave** | Thin lines crossing and weaving in slow motion, forming patterns | Minimalist branding, architecture firms |

**Color themes to produce for EACH:** Dark navy + cyan, Black + gold, Dark + white, Black + neon purple, Dark green + mint, Warm dark + orange

**Duration:** 15-20 seconds, seamless loop | **Resolution:** 4K

### 3. Technology / HUD / Cyber Overlays

**Specific videos to produce:**

| # | Video Theme | Visual Description | Who Buys It |

| 3 | **Data Matrix Rain** | Columns of falling characters (green, cyan, or white) | Cybersecurity content, hacker aesthetic |
| 4 | **Circuit Trace** | PCB traces lighting up progressively, nodes glowing | Electronics, semiconductor, hardware companies |
| 5 | **Holographic Grid Floor** | Perspective grid stretching to horizon with scan lines | Sci-fi, product launches, futuristic branding |

| 7 | **Fingerprint Scan** | Fingerprint outline with scanning line and "ACCESS GRANTED" text | Security companies, identity verification |
| 8 | **World Map Network** | Globe or flat map with pulsing connection lines between cities | Global business, logistics, telecom |

**Color themes:** Cyan on dark, Green on dark (military), Orange on dark (alert), White on dark (clean)

**Duration:** 15 seconds loop | **Resolution:** 4K + transparent WebM versions for overlays

### 6. Kinetic Typography / Text Animations

High volume — every content creator needs these.

**Specific videos to produce:**

| # | Video Theme | Visual Description | Who Buys It |
|---|------------|-------------------|-------------|
| 1 | **Word-by-Word Reveal** | Each word fades/slides in one at a time, clean and modern | Presentation intros, motivational content |

## Stock Metadata Strategy

**Title formula:** `[Adjective] [Color] [Subject] [Type] | [Resolution]`
- Example: "Abstract Blue Hexagonal Grid Background Loop | 4K"
- Example: "Futuristic Cyan HUD Overlay with Scan Lines | 4K"

**Keywords per video:** 25-50 tags covering:
- Visual elements (particles, gradient, geometric, hexagon)
- Color (blue, cyan, neon, dark, gold)
- Mood (futuristic, elegant, minimal, corporate, cinematic)
- Use case (background, overlay, intro, presentation, website)
- Technical (loop, seamless, 4K, motion graphics, animated)
- Industry (technology, business, science, medical, finance)
